>>>  SmartDocs is an intelligent PDF assistant designed to make document interaction seamless and efficient.

With SmartDocs, users can upload PDF documents and instantly ask questions based on the content, receiving precise and context-aware answers.

SmartDocs processes and extracts meaningful text from PDFs, splits the information into manageable chunks, and delivers accurate responses through its AI-powered query system.

At its core, SmartDocs uses the Llama 3.1 models for querying and FAISS for fast and reliable vector search, combined with the highly efficient MiniLM-L6-v2 embeddings.

Whether you're a student, professional, or researcher, SmartDocs is your go-to tool for navigating large documents effortlessly, saving time, and increasing productivity.
